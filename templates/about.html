About Conquistador

Serving Central Pennsylvania since 2004

Our Story

Conquistador Oil, Heating & Air Conditioning Inc. has been serving the Lancaster County community since 2004. Based at 931 N Shippen Street in Lancaster, PA, we've built our reputation on connecting homeowners and businesses with reliable, qualified contractors for all their heating oil and HVAC needs.

Our Mission

We believe every home deserves reliable heating and cooling. Our mission is to make it easy for Central PA residents to find trustworthy, licensed contractors who deliver quality service at fair prices.

How We Vet Our Contractors

Every contractor in our network goes through a rigorous vetting process:

  • Valid PA contractor license (HVAC) or fuel dealer permit (oil delivery)
  • General liability insurance ($1M minimum) and workers' compensation
  • Minimum 2 years in business
  • Minimum 3.5 star average on review platforms
  • No unresolved BBB complaints
  • Commitment to our service standards and response times

Quality Guarantee

After every service, we follow up with customers to ensure satisfaction. Contractors are rated on timeliness, professionalism, and problem resolution. Those who consistently deliver excellent service receive priority lead routing, while those who fall short are counseled or removed from our network.
