About Conquistador

Serving Central Pennsylvania since 2004

Our Story

Conquistador Oil, Heating & Air Conditioning Inc. has been serving the Lancaster County community since 2004. Based at 931 N Shippen Street in Lancaster, PA, we've built our reputation on delivering reliable, professional heating oil and HVAC services to homeowners and businesses throughout Central Pennsylvania.

Our Mission

We believe every home deserves reliable heating and cooling. Our mission is to deliver quality service at fair prices with the fastest response times in Central PA.

Our Standards

We hold ourselves to the highest industry standards:

  • PA licensed (HVAC) and permitted (oil delivery)
  • Fully insured with general liability and workers' compensation
  • Over 20 years of experience serving Central PA
  • Background-checked, professional technicians
  • BBB accredited with no unresolved complaints
  • Guaranteed response times for every service call

Quality Guarantee

After every service, we follow up with customers to ensure satisfaction. Our technicians are held to strict standards on timeliness, professionalism, and problem resolution. If you're not satisfied, we'll make it right.
